This website is for managing a micro library. The goal is to provide a graphical front-end for tracking available books and associating them with relevant users if they are checked out. Users must register to use this platform by entering a valid username and email address. Once they are inside of the system, they can browse the available books and check them out in-person with the help of a registered librarian. Books are automatically leased for a pre-specified number of days and emails are sent out to users whose books are coming due. Optionally, users can extend their lease for a specified number of days not to exceed the maximum lease time.

Having said that, the platform is purely experimental and was developed for my own personal use and published for others to appreciate.

Browse the wiki to learn more about this platform and how it can be used.
